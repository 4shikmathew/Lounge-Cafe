Our Story

Lounge Café was founded by two college friends who shared a simple idea and a big dream. During their college days, they spent countless hours discussing life, plans, and what they wanted to build in the future. One dream stayed with them — creating a peaceful café space where people could relax, connect, and enjoy good food without the rush of everyday life.

After graduating, they decided to bring that dream to life. Each friend brought their own strengths: one with a passion for experimenting with flavors and crafting signature drinks, and the other with a talent for creating warm, comfortable, and aesthetic environments. Together, they transformed their shared vision into Lounge Café, a place filled with calm vibes, thoughtful design, and quality in every cup.

At Lounge Café, every detail is created with care — cozy seating, soft lighting, soothing music, and a menu that matches every mood. It’s a space made for slow moments, deep conversations, quiet work sessions, and breaks from busy routines.

We believe a café should feel like a comfortable corner of your life — familiar, welcoming, and refreshing. That’s why we focus on fresh ingredients, personal service, and an atmosphere that makes you want to stay a little longer.

Built by two friends from the same college, Lounge Café is more than a business — it’s a story of friendship, passion, and a shared vision turned into reality.
